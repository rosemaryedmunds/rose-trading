SETUP 01
10-Min Open Range Breakout
Condition
Break AND retest of OR High or Low after 9:40am ET. Mark the range 9:30–9:40am.
Entry
On the RETEST of the broken level — not the initial break candle.
Stop / Target
Stop: OR midpoint. Target: next ATR level / S&R / momentum shift.
✓ Candle close confirmation before entry
✗ Entering on the initial break candle
✗ Trading before 9:40am
SETUP 02
Vomy (Bearish Ribbon Transition)
Anatomy
Clear trending ribbon → fins at resistance → 8/13 break → 13 EMA retest → 48 EMA break confirmed.
Entry
Early: 13 EMA retest (tight stop). Confirmed: 48 EMA break & hold = A-level.
Ultimate Scenario
48 break + active compression + RSI/Phase at extreme.
✓ Wait for 48 EMA break for confirmation
✗ Entering before candle close
SETUP 03
iVomy (Bullish Ribbon Transition)
Condition
Same as Vomy but bullish. Fin forms at support (Put Trigger). 48 EMA break upward confirms.
Entry / Stop
Entry on 48 EMA break close or pullback to 13 EMA. Stop: other side of ribbon.
✓ 48 EMA break = confirmed entry
✓ Check conviction arrow (13/48 cross)
✗ Chasing after ribbon is far away
SETUP 04
Flag Into Ribbon
Condition
Trending market (ribbon stacked). Price pulls back INTO the ribbon which acts as support/resistance.
Entry / Stop
Entry at 13 or 21 EMA. Conviction arrow + pullback = high conviction. Stop: other side of ribbon.
✓ Wicks AT ribbon (not through it) = good retest
✗ Wicks THROUGH ribbon = bad retest, skip
SETUP 05
Divergence From Extreme
Condition
Range market. Phase Oscillator in Distribution (≥61.8) or Accumulation (≤-61.8). Class A divergence preferred.
Entry / Stop
Entry on CANDLE CLOSE confirmation only. Stop: above/below swing H/L. Target: 21 EMA → ribbon.
✓ Class A = price lower low + osc higher low
✗ Class C divergence = low conviction
✗ Osc in neutral zone = not valid
SETUP 06
1-Min EOD Divergence
Condition
ONLY 3:00–4:00pm ET. Phase Oscillator divergence + volume confirmation at swing H/L.
Entry / Stop
Entry on candle close. Stop: above/below swing. Target: mean reversion to 21 EMA.
✓ Volume confirmation required
✗ Outside 3–4pm window = invalid
SETUP 07
Tweezer Bottom "Wicky Wicky"
Condition
Two parallel candles with matching bottom wicks at support level.
Entry / Stop
Entry: 50% reclaim of down candle. Stop: below wicks → trail to body lows → trail below structure.
✓ Wicks must be parallel and matching
✗ Entering before 50% reclaim
