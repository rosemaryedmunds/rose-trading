# Week 2: The Direction – Do Not Fight the River

In Week 1 we learned how to spot energy compression using Bar Types and the 2-1-2 setup. That tells us *when* a move is likely coming. Now we need to answer the more important question: **which way?**

The answer lives above you — on higher timeframes. The river already has a direction. Our only job is to figure out which way it's flowing and swim with it.

---

## Time Frame Continuity (TFC)

The most common — and most costly — mistake new traders make is shorting a stock while the Monthly, Weekly, and Daily charts are all trending higher. You are trying to stop a freight train with your bare hands.

**The Rule:** We only trade in the direction of the Aggregate Timeframe.

* If the Monthly is Green, we look for Buys.
* If the Monthly is Red, we look for Sells.
* If the Monthly is inside/grey, we wait for clarity.

Each timeframe is a vote. The more votes aligned in one direction, the higher your probability of success.

### The Top-Down Review (Every Morning)

Run this before looking at any setup:

1. **Monthly chart** — Is the ribbon green, red, or grey? This is the master direction.
2. **Weekly chart** — Does it agree with Monthly? Confirmation before proceeding.
3. **Daily chart** — What bar type is today's candle? Inside = compression brewing. 2U = bullish session. 2D = bearish session.
4. **4H chart** — What is the morning bias? This is your session direction.
5. **Entry timeframe** — Now, and only now, look for a setup in the confirmed direction.

> If the Monthly is Red and you are trying to buy — **you are the liquidity.** You are the exit for smart money that went short above you. Full alignment protects you from this fate.

---

## The Ribbon (Saty's Pivot Ribbon)

We use Saty's Pivot Ribbon — a moving average structure that color-codes the dominant trend — to visualize the direction of the river on any timeframe.

### Green Ribbon — Trend is UP
* Only look for **bullish setups**: 2-1-2↑ breakouts, 2-2↑ reversals.
* Selling Puts is your primary income strategy.
* Counter-trend shorts are forbidden.

### Red Ribbon — Trend is DOWN
* Only look for **bearish setups**: 2-1-2↓ breakdowns, 2-2↓ reversals.
* Bearish spreads and selling Calls come into play.
* Never "buy the dip" until the ribbon shows a clear flip.

### Grey / Twisting Ribbon — No Man's Land
* The market is confused. No clear trend exists.
* **Stay out.** This is the Kill Zone for your account.
* A twisting ribbon does not mean "trade both directions" — it means no trades at all.

> **Sniper Tip:** A twisting or grey ribbon is not a mystery to solve — it is a stop sign. The traders who ignore it are providing liquidity to everyone else. When the ribbon is twisted, your edge disappears. Wait. Do not analyze. Just wait.

---

## Do This. Never Do This.

**Do this:**
* Monthly + Weekly green → look for long setups only
* Check ribbon color before every single trade, every single day
* Wait for ribbon to show clear direction before entering
* Size full when all timeframes agree

**Never do this:**
* Short when Monthly + Weekly are both green
* Trade during a grey/twisting ribbon period
* Make a directional decision based on one timeframe alone
* Assume ribbon color without actually looking at the chart

---

## The 30-Second Sanity Check

Before any trade, ask yourself this question:

> *"If I held this position for 30 days, would the river carry me or fight me?"*

If the monthly ribbon is green and price is above structure, the river carries you. If monthly is red or grey, the river fights you. Every trade taken against the monthly ribbon requires you to be right about direction, timing, and magnitude simultaneously — and probability starts working against you.
